# TimeFormatter

<ComponentStatus stage="pre" responsive="no" accessible="no" />

In Jobber a TimeFormatter is used to ensure that time is displayed in the
expected format. 12 hour vs. 24 hour time.

## Simple Example

<Playground>
  <TimeFormatter civilTime={"11:30:00.000000000"} use24HourClock={false} />
  <br />
  <TimeFormatter civilTime={"17:45:00.000000000"} use24HourClock={false} />
</Playground>

## 24 hour Example

<Playground>
  <TimeFormatter civilTime={"17:45:00.000000000"} use24HourClock={true} />
</Playground>
